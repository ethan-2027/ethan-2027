About Civic Unfiltered

We're a student-run political blog that believes democracy works best when everyone has a voice—especially young people who will inherit the consequences of today's political decisions.

Founded by students, for students, Civic Unfiltered breaks down the barriers between complex political issues and the generation that will be most affected by them. We don't just report on politics; we engage with it, question it, and sometimes roast it.

Our mission is simple: make political discourse accessible, engaging, and relevant to Gen Z and college students everywhere. No corporate spin, no ancient talking points—just real perspectives from real students.
